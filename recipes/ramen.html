Ramen Noodles

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodle packet
  • pot
  • egg

Directions

  1. Heat like 5 cups of water in the pot to boiling
  2. Open the Ramen pack a put in only the noodels, set the foil flavor pack aside for later
  3. Wait about 3 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and drain most of the water off
  5. Open and stir in foil flavor packet
  6. Crack egg into soup while the water is still hot enough to cook it
  7. Stir a little bit
  8. Wait until cool and eat, perferably with chop sticks directly from the pan
