Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Box of mac and cheese
  • milk
  • butter

Directions

  1. Boil like 8 cups of water
  2. Put in macaroni noodels
  3. Cook for about 10 minutes
  4. Drain water
  5. Add powdered cheese first add mix
  6. Spalsh in a tiny bit of milk and a pad of butter
  7. Mix well
  8. Enjoy from pan with a side of canned salmon or tuna
