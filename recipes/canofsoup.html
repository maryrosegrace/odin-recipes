Can of Soup

Ingredients

  • can of soup
  • pot
  • spoon
  • Optional Ingredient:bowl

Directions

  1. Open soup can
  2. Pour soup into pot
  3. Turn on stove and heat to disired tempurature
  4. Turn off stove
  5. Pour pot contents into bowl if you want to get real fancy
  6. Eat directly from the pot if you want to cut down on dishes
